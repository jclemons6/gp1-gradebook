How to load your Snowflake production data
STEP 1 — OPEN SNOWFLAKE
Log into your Snowflake account and open a new worksheet.
STEP 2 — RUN THE QUERY BELOW
Paste the query below. Update table/column names to match your Snowflake schema. Names must match your roster exactly.
STEP 3 — EXPORT AS CSV
Click the download button in Snowflake results and choose CSV. Save the file to your computer.
STEP 4 — UPLOAD HERE
Click "Upload CSV from Snowflake" above and select the file. Repeat daily for fresh data.
YOUR SNOWFLAKE QUERY — update table/column names to match your schema:
SELECT
  e.EMPLOYEE_NAME AS "Name",
  -- App metrics vs median
  p.APPS_TOTAL AS "Apps",
  m.MEDIAN_APPS AS "Company_Median_Apps",
  ROUND(p.APPS_TOTAL/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_APPS,0)*100) AS "Apps_vs_Median_Pct",
  p.APPS_FUNDED AS "Apps_Funded",
  -- Loan metrics vs median
  p.LOANS_TOTAL AS "Loans",
  m.MEDIAN_LOANS AS "Company_Median_Loans",
  ROUND(p.LOANS_TOTAL/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_LOANS,0)*100) AS "Loans_vs_Median_Pct",
  p.LOANS_SMALL AS "Loans_Small",
  p.LOANS_LARGE AS "Loans_Large",
  p.LOANS_AUTO AS "Loans_Auto",
  m.MEDIAN_AUTO AS "Company_Median_Auto",
  -- Volume vs median
  p.VOLUME_FINANCED AS "Volume_Financed",
  m.MEDIAN_VOLUME AS "Company_Median_Volume",
  ROUND(p.VOLUME_FINANCED/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_VOLUME,0)*100) AS "Volume_vs_Median_Pct",
  p.AUTO_VOLUME AS "Auto_Volume",
  -- Insurance vs median
  p.INSURANCE_PREMIUM AS "Insurance_Premium",
  m.MEDIAN_INSURANCE AS "Company_Median_Insurance",
  -- Performance tier
  CASE
    WHEN p.APPS_TOTAL=0 AND p.LOANS_TOTAL=0 THEN 'NO ACTIVITY'
    WHEN ROUND(p.LOANS_TOTAL/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_LOANS,0)*100) < 70 THEN 'BELOW MEDIAN'
    WHEN ROUND(p.LOANS_TOTAL/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_LOANS,0)*100) < 90 THEN 'SLIGHTLY BELOW'
    WHEN ROUND(p.LOANS_TOTAL/NULLIF(m.MEDIAN_LOANS,0)*100) < 120 THEN 'AT MEDIAN'
    ELSE 'ABOVE MEDIAN'
  END AS "vs_Company"

FROM EMPLOYEES e
JOIN PRODUCTION_METRICS p ON e.EMPLOYEE_ID = p.EMPLOYEE_ID
JOIN COMPANY_MEDIANS m ON m.PERIOD = p.PERIOD
WHERE e.EMPLOYEE_NAME IN (

  'Austin Jones', 'Carrie Turner', 'Christina Moore', 'Cynthia Lewis',
  'Elizabeth Garcia', 'Francisco Lares', 'Ginger Gonzalez', 'Gustavo Hernandez Lara',
  'Gwen Jenkins', 'Jaime Anaya Raya', 'Jakob Cervantes', 'Johanna Arana',
  'Karen Thomas', 'Martha Santos', 'Monica Crew', 'Rasha Tyler',

)
ORDER BY e.EMPLOYEE_NAME;
